Predicting Blood Group
through Eye Image Analysis

Non-Invasive Diagnostic Machine Learning

Our system uses advanced machine learning techniques to analyze retinal fundus and sclera images, providing accurate and non-invasive blood group predictions.

By detecting subtle patterns in vascular structures and scleral coloration, our model delivers rapid and reliable results without the need for traditional blood tests.

Capabilities

Our Prediction Capabilities

DATA PREPROCESSING

We clean, normalize, and enhance retinal and scleral images to ensure optimal quality for analysis.

FEATURE EXTRACTION

Our algorithms detect vessel patterns, tortuosity, and scleral pigmentation to derive meaningful features.

BLOOD GROUP PREDICTION

Using a trained deep learning model, we combine extracted features to accurately predict the user’s blood group.

TRAINING & ANALYSIS

We generate detailed performance metrics including accuracy graphs, confusion matrices, and precision-recall charts.
